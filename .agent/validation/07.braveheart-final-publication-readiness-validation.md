4. Verified testimonial publication pending for case studies.
5. HomeToHome app URL stubbed (`CTA_CONFIG.appUrl` null).
6. Verification backend / CRM not connected (Phase 1 by design; blocks live intake).
7. Founder narrative and legacy timeline editorial approval pending (TBD placeholders on Our Story).
8. Staging/preview hosts need `Disallow: /` robots override at deploy time.
