## Modal / Dialog Behavior

- `role="dialog"` + `aria-modal="true"` + labelled title/description
- Escape key closes modal
- Backdrop click closes modal
- Body scroll locked while open
- Focus moves to close button on open
- Close button has `aria-label`
- Success state after submit (front-end only)
- Form resets on close

## Form Fields Implemented

1. Full name (required)
2. Email (required)
3. Phone (optional)
4. Service category (required select)
5. Active / retired / status (required select)
6. Buying / selling / both / undecided (required select)
7. State or market area (required text)
8. Message (optional textarea)
