## What verification means in Phase 1

Brave Heart uses a structured review process to understand whether a household may qualify for program participation. During Phase 1, the website collects intake information through a front-end form only. Submitting the form starts a review request; it does not confirm eligibility or guarantee benefits.

## What participants should expect

Eligible categories may include public-safety professionals, military members, veterans, and related household members, subject to program rules and provider participation. A program team member may follow up after review when backend processing is available.

## Helpful next steps

- Review the [Who We Serve](/who-we-serve) page for category context.
- Use the Verify Eligibility entry point when you are ready to submit a review request.
- Read the program disclaimer on benefit pages for transaction-specific limitations.

Verification data handling is described in the draft [Privacy Policy](/privacy-policy), which remains subject to legal review.
