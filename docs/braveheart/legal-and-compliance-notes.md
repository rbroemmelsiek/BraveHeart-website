# Brave Heart Legal and Compliance Notes

## Purpose

Tracks content areas requiring careful review before publication.

## High-Sensitivity Claims

The following require review:

- brokerage commission credits;
- lender credits;
- escrow/title discounts;
- inspection discounts;
- NHD discounts;
- home warranty credits;
- 1031 exchange discounts;
- RESPA alignment;
- eligibility categories;
- verification processes;
- privacy/data security claims;
- nationwide availability;
- provider network claims;
- guarantee/accountability language.

## Publication Rule

Do not publish expanded or newly invented claims in these categories without review.

## Safer Language Pattern

Use:

```text
Eligible participants may receive...
Credits and discounts are subject to final verification, provider availability, and transaction-specific rules.
```

Avoid:

```text
You are guaranteed...
Everyone receives...
We always provide...
```

## Placeholder Disclaimer

Add a concise disclaimer near benefits content until legal copy is finalized:

```text
Program benefits are subject to eligibility verification, provider availability, transaction terms, lender approval, and applicable law. Final credits and discounts are confirmed during the transaction process.
```
